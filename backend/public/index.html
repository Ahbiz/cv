How to Install

1

Download the Extension Zip

Click the download button above or grab the latest `AutoFillEngine.zip` package from your shared source folder.

2

Enable Chrome Developer Mode

Open **Chrome**, go to chrome://extensions in your URL bar, and turn on the **Developer mode** toggle in the top-right corner.

3

Load the Extension

Unzip the folder. In Chrome, click the **Load unpacked** button in the top-left, and select the extracted extension folder.

4

Upload and Fill

Pin the **AF** icon to your toolbar. Click it, select your resume file, click parse, and navigate to any job application form to fill instantly!
